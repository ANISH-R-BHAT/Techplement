Commands:

1) To create the quiz- createQuiz quiz_name
2) To add a question- addQuestion quiz_name "question" "option 1" "option 2" "option 3" "option 4" opttion_no
3) To take the quiz- takeQuiz quiz_name
4) To exit- exit

Example:

createQuiz TechQuiz

addQuestion Tech "What was the fastest growing web browser in 2020?" "Microsoft Edge" "Firefox" "DuckDuckGo" "Internet Explorer" 1

addQuestion Tech "What does SMS stand for?" "Short meter service" "Share more stories" "Secure messages service" "Short message service" 4

takeQuiz TechQuiz

exit
